Howdy, Y'all!

Welcome to Steve's Country Favorites! This site is where I share my love of country music including: my favorite artists, songs, and concerts I have attended over the years.

My love for country music started at a really young and I have been a fan ever since. I have many favorite artists - Some include: the lead singer of Hootie & the Blowfish turned country superstar Darius Rucker, Brad Paisley, Ashley McBryde, Carly Pearce and many more.




There are many country songs I have enjoyed listening to over the years, even as the country genre has evolved from traditional sounding with the Carter Family (1927 - 1956) to more of a pop music blending with the remix of Meant to Be by Florida Georgia Line featuring pop singer Bebe Rexha (2018), and everything in between.
